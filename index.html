About

Mechanical Engineer  •  Business Analyst  •  Software Engineer



Hi, I’m Jasen—a passionate and multidisciplinary professional with a deep interest in engineering, business, and software development. My journey has allowed me to merge technical expertise with business acumen, giving me a unique perspective in tackling complex problems from both a strategic and engineering-focused lens.

I’m driven by curiosity and a desire to continuously learn, whether it’s diving into the intricacies of mechanical systems or exploring the vast landscape of software development. Currently, I’m working on projects that combine my varied skill set, including developing a website that connects artists, venues, and shows, helping creatives and businesses thrive.

In addition to my technical background, I enjoy designing efficient and clean user experiences, and I’m a big fan of minimalist design principles. In my free time, I’m always experimenting with new ideas in both engineering and technology, striving to create solutions that make an impact.
